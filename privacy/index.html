Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. What We Collect

When you use RIVL, we collect:

  • Account information (name, email address)
  • Workout data (activity type, duration, distance, heart rate)
  • Competition and campaign participation data
  • Data from connected third-party services (Strava, Garmin Connect) when you choose to link them

2. Third-Party Integrations

When you connect Strava or Garmin Connect, we receive:

  • Activity summaries (type, duration, distance)
  • We do NOT access your full activity history — only new activities created after you connect

You can disconnect these services at any time from your profile settings. Upon disconnection, we stop receiving new data but retain previously synced activity logs.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Track workout progress in competitions and campaigns
  • Display leaderboards and team standings
  • Enable validation of completed sessions
  • Synchronize activity data between connected services

4. Data Storage

Data is stored securely via Supabase (hosted on AWS in the EU). Access is controlled through row-level security policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell or share your personal data with third parties. Other users can only see:

  • Your display name
  • Competition results you participate in

6. Your Rights

You can at any time:

  • Export your data (from Profile → Settings)
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Disconnect third-party integrations

7. Contact

Questions about privacy? Contact us at contact@getrivl.app.
